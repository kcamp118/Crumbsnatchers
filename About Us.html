    About Us Page

    Crumbsnatchers, owned and operated by Dee Campbell, started 2009 in Greensboro NC, Crumbsnatchers provides specially baked treats and cakes. Our custom baked sweets and treats are made using non-toxic, organic ingredients.

    .

    We bake delicious fun cupcakes, cakes, cookies from scratch such as our red velvet cakes, cookies and cream, vanilla and so many other flavors.
